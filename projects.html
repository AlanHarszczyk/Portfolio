Overview:

An Phiast Destruction is a top-down action game created for a Dingle game jam, where I was Lead Creator and Lead Artist.
Players control An Phiast, a monster wreaking havoc on a city based on real-world Dingle, aiming for a high score before being defeated.
The game features automatic attacks, upgrades, and destruction-based scoring, rewarding speed and efficiency.
Completed within the time limit, it delivers fast-paced, strategic chaos with immersive visuals.
