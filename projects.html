An Phiast Destruction

Overview:

An Phiast Destruction is a top-down action game created for a Dingle game jam, where I was Lead Creator and Lead Artist.
Players control An Phiast, a monster wreaking havoc on a city based on real-world Dingle, aiming for a high score before being defeated.
The game features automatic attacks, upgrades, and destruction-based scoring, rewarding speed and efficiency.
Completed within the time limit, it delivers fast-paced, strategic chaos with immersive visuals.

Echoes Of Eira

Overview:

Echoes of Eira is a game about fighting for home and heritage.
You play as a Celtic warrior in mythological Ireland, caught in the struggle against invading British forces.
But it’s not just about the battles—you’re also building a village, gathering allies, and forging a future for your people.
Along the way, you’ll upgrade your weapons and armor, trade with merchants for rare items, and make choices that shape the world around you.
With rich Celtic lore and a beautifully crafted world, Echoes of Eira brings history and myth together in an epic journey of survival, strategy, and resilience

Deck Of Policy

Overview:

Deck Of Policy is a game focused on resource management.
You are the new town mayor and its upto you to run this city.
Utilise decks of cards influencing your resources, how your town develops alongside your final score.
Utilise the advisors giving new buffs and upgrades.
